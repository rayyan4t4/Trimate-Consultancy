Our Story

Founded in Peshawar with a simple goal, Trimat Consultancy was established to bridge the gap between ambitious individuals and global opportunities. Our founders, having navigated the complexities of immigration themselves, wanted to create a transparent and supportive consultancy. We've since grown into a trusted name, helping hundreds of students and professionals from Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and beyond achieve their dreams of studying and working in countries like **Germany, Romania, Canada, and the USA**.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower our clients by providing ethical, reliable, and affordable visa guidance. We are committed to simplifying the application process, ensuring you receive the best possible support on your journey.

Our vision is to be the most respected immigration consultancy in the region, known for our high success rate and unwavering dedication to our clients' futures.
