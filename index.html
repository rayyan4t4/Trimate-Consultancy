Romania & Georgia - Student Visa

Total Cost

14–15 lakh PKR

Advance Payment

1.5 lakh PKR (Registration, appointment ,insurance etc )

Documents Required:

  • Secondary School Certificate + DMC
  • Higher Secondary School Certificate + DMC
  • FRC (Family Registration Certificate) + Birth Certificate
  • Police Clearance Certificate
  • For Master's applicants (additional):
  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate + Transcript

Note:

All documents must be attested by IBCC and MOFA, and must have an apostille.

Germany - Open Work Permit (Done Base)

Total Cost

48 lakh PKR

Advance Payment

5 lakh PKR

Processing Time

6 months

Visa Duration

1–2 years

Requirements:

  • Valid passport
  • Professional qualifications or work experience
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Proof of financial means
  • Clean criminal record
